Corn Tchap

Description

Here is a beautiful Cameroonian recipe! Corn tchap is a succulent preparation made from red beans, fish and corn.

Ingredients

  • 200g red kidney beans
  • 2 fish fillets
  • 2 onions
  • 1 small piece of ginger
  • 200g canned corn
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Place the cooked bean in a large saucepan. Add corn and vegetable oil. Mix.
  2. Peel and finely chop the onions
  3. Dice the tomatoes.
  4. Add the onions, tomatoes, fish, ginger, a little salt and pepper to the pan containing the beans and corn.
  5. Cover with water and simmer for 25 minutes, stirring regularly. If needed, add a little extra water.
  6. Adjust the seasoning if necessary and serve immediately.

Coconut Rice

Description

A make-ahead basmati side dish with mild coconut flavors.

Ingredients

  • 3 onions
  • 1 tablespoons sunflower oil
  • 1 tablespoons butter
  • 1kg bag basmati rice
  • 2 x 400ml cans coconut milk

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop the onions
  2. In a big saucepan or casserole with a lid, gently soften the onions in the oil and butter
  3. When really soft, add the rice and cook, stirring, for a couple of mins.
  4. Tip in the coconut milk with 2 cans of water. Bring to a gentle simmer, stirring, then cover, lower the heat to the lowest setting and cook for 10-15 mins.
    (Scraping the bottom regularly with a wooden spoon to stop it sticking)
  5. Check the rice: it should be sticky and creamy but not watery, and not quite cooked â€“ still a bit crunchy. Tip into a big bowl that will fit in a microwave and cover with cling film.
    (Chill until ready to serve, for up to a day.)
  6. To serve, poke a hole in the cling film and microwave for 8 mins on High, stirring halfway, until piping hot and cooked through. Add some salt and stir with a fork to break up the grains.
